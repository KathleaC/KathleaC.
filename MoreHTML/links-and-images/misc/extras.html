HTML Entities

There are three reserved characters in HTML: < (less than), > (greater than), and & (ampersand). To use these characters in your HTML, you need to use HTML entities: &lt;, &gt;, and &amp;. One should always use these entities instead of the reserved characters when writing HTML.
